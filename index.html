Amazing Swedish Massage

Tunza Spa & Wellness therapists use a sequence of long, gliding strokes, kneads, and circular

motions to help ease shallow tension in the muscles. Aromatherapy may be added to enhance relaxation.
Besides helping to relax the muscles and ease superficial tension, Swedish massage may also help boost circulation throughout the body.

Deep Tissue Massage

As its name suggests, deep-tissue massage aims to relieve tension

in the deeper layers of muscle tissue and fascia. Because of this, it uses more intense kneading techniques than Swedish.Tunza Spa therapists are exemplary at this ensuring release of chronic tension and help improve range of motion.

Hot Stone Massage

Rather than relying solely on their hands to work out stubborn knots,

the massage therapists at Tunza Spa & Wellness place heated basalt stones on the back to help melt tight areas,helping to relieve tense areas and improving blood flow.

Sports Massage

Tunza Spa Therapists have specifically designed this massage

with athletes in mind, sports massage is a variation on Swedish massage that aims to address aches caused by repetitive motion and help active people make a quicker recovery from stress and injury.

Herbal Massage

Herbal Compress Massage boosts emotional well-being

while soothing aching muscles and stiff joints. Sticking to traditional techniques, a muslin cloth is infused with herbs and spices and then steamed to release their medicinal properties.
Each therapist uses their own blend of herbs, but a mix of lemongrass, turmeric, kaffir lime leaves and tamarind are our favourites.

Reflexology Massage

Reflexology is a specialized form of foot massage, during which,

Tunza Spa practitioners stimulate precise points on the feet that are believed to correlate to other organs and bodily systems.
Traditionally, reflexology is believed to help correct imbalances throughout the body. But like any foot massage, itâ€™s also great for relieving tension brought on by wearing high heels or being on your feet all day.
